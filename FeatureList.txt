Features of the System (Prioritized)
1.	Generates groups for class project. 
2.	Can retrieve fundamental information from the university registrar.
3.	Collects instructor’s and student’s information and preferences with regards to grouping.
4.	Offers option for instructor to approve or modify final group listing.
5.	Ensures Instructor’s preferences override student’s preferences where such conflicts arise.(part of the group generation method hence implemented as a part of use case 4)
6.	Prompts instructor and student to enter their information and preferences.
7.	Provides the option to edit previously entered information for both instructor and students.
8.	Highlights conflicts that could not be resolved during group generation so that such conflicts are clear to instructor.(part of group generation method/ handled by group class and instructor may provide approval or modification input)
9.	Notifies students of group listing once finalized by instructor.
10.	System accounts for student’s schedules and preferences.(part of grouping algorithm hence implemented as a part of use case 1 and 6)
11.	Capable of balancing groups in terms of skill for some projects given the students submit additional information.(part of group generation method hence implemented as a part of use case 1)
